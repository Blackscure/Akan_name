Description About The Ghanian Culture

Akan is the language of the people called akan.Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born It is a cultural event held with high esteem and passed to tradition. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person
